In the drama series Monica, the good girl mask comes off as Brenda plays the titular role of Monica, an ambitious social climber from the ghetto who will do anything to become part of the elite of South City – the fictitious town that Monica is set in. Monica which is directed by Neil Schell (who has previously directed other Kenyan hits like Rush and Saints) and produced by Scolly Cheruto follows Monica’s journey to success, and everyone caught in the middle of it all, either knowingly or unknowingly.
