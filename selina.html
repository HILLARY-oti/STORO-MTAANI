Characters

Selina, in pursuit of evidence to prove her innocence, has no option but to go an extra mile. It is a bumpy ride as she finds herself in between love and her quest.


Luke Mckenzie


Selina

Selina, a 20-year-old girl, is brought to a wealthy city home by her two-faced (super-sweet on the outside, cunning within), rural stepmother Kristina who has brainwashed her innocent mind into believing she needs to work here to support her family, her unwell father, & her younger step-brother and sister.


Nelson

While he’s a warm character, his focus yet isn’t his family at all, but his torrid relationship with another super-hot, super-eligible girl from a wealthy family of well-known cosmetic surgeon parents.


Patricia

She comes from a moneyed, politically well-connected family & the house - as well as the business - are still in her name. Therefore, she has the upper hand in the running of the house - outwardly at least.

Kristina is simply looking to make some easy money from Selina. She has lied to Zakayo (her husband) that she’s sending Selina off to a family that is willing to sponsor her studies & that it’ll be good for the girl.
